About Me

Hello! I’m Dr. Liyuan Liu, currently an Assistant Professor at Saint Joseph’s University. I have authored and co-authored 30 peer-reviewed articles, focusing on areas like AI algorithms in various applications—marketing, healthcare, finance, human resource management, and education. My research also delves into incentive mechanisms design in the big data era, data security, privacy, and blockchain technology.

Outside the university, I engage in music, playing the accordion for over 20 years and enjoying piano duets with my son, Noah. I also cherish playing Majiang, writing Chinese prose, and dancing. My favorite book, "The Crowd," explores the complexities of human behavior, mirroring the depth of my research interests.

For more about my scholarly work, please visit my Google Scholar profile.
